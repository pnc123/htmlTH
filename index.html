Maths Formulas



Compound Interest Formula

Simple Interest = (P × R × T) ÷ 100

Amount = SI + P

A = {(P × R × T) ÷ 100} + P

Where

SI Simple Interest

A Amount/Future Value

P Principal Amount

R Rate of Interest per annum

T Time in years
